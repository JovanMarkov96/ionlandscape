Paul Haljan is an Associate Professor of Physics at Simon Fraser University, where he leads the Trapped Ion Quantum Technology Lab. His research focuses on trapped-ion quantum technologies, including scalable quantum computing with cadmium and ytterbium ions, microstructure development for ion trap designs, and high-fidelity entanglement operations.
