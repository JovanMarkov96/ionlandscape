Dr. Christian F. Roos is an experimental physicist working on trapped-ion quantum science, including quantum simulation and precision spectroscopy. He is affiliated with IQOQI Innsbruck (Austrian Academy of Sciences) and the University of Innsbruck.

Roos completed his PhD at the University of Innsbruck under Prof. Rainer Blatt, with a thesis on controlling the quantum state of trapped ions. His research has contributed to foundational trapped-ion techniques for coherent control, entangling dynamics, and precision measurement.
